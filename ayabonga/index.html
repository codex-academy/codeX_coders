Who Is AYA ?

Ayabonga Benedicta Qwabi known by many names such as Salmone and Billyboy is a young computer enthusiast from Cape Town. He was born and raised in Lady Frere South Africa .He is one of the two children of Tamara Qwabi , a phenomenal woman from Queenstown who passed away in the year 2005. In 2007 he moved to the small bigtown Dutywa located near uMthatha in the Eastern Cape and there on he lived his life untill today. There he made friends with a large number of people but one closer to him the most was Masande Dudula from Qumbu near uMthatha .He met Masande in the year 2008 while he was doing his 10th grade in Mida English Medium Christian School. In the year 2011 he became the Headboy (Head Prefect) of Mida school , He also got the honour of being in the first group of students to do CAT(Computer Applications Technology) as a subject at Mida EMCS . He also became the only student to pass the subject with a distinction at grade 12 thus becoming the first tsudent to pass it with a distinction in the Dutywa district.
